My nickname is Alle. I have two children. My four and a half years old son Elis and my two year old daughter Ida. My parter is a skilled mother, graphic designer, painter, illustrator and a wonderful person.

I have three sisters. A doctor, a psychologist and a criminologist. It's easy to see that the my siblings have found different areas of interests in life. I believe I add to that diversity by being in love with subjects such as quantum mechanics, astronomy, geology, anthropology, DNA and ancient-history.

If I ever find room for it, I've been known to read "The Wheel of Time".
